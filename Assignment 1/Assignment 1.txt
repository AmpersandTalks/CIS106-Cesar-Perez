How to make an egg sandwich
1. Get  loaf of bread
2. take out eggs
3. take out cheese
4. take out frying pan
5. turn on stove
6. take out oil
7. pour oil on empty frying pan
8. crack 2 eggs
9. pour eggs on to the frying pan
10. take out toaster 
11. put two slices of bread on toaster to bake
12. scramble eggs on pan
13. take out bread from toaster
14. take out plate
15. put slices of bread on plate
16. put slice of cheese on bread
17. put egg on bread
18. close sandwich 
19. enjoy
